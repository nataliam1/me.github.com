For Our Father Founder Under thy care O Lord, we entrust our father founder. Very Rev. Fr. Emmanuel M.P Edeh. Please Lord shower your unending blessing on Him, so that he will be able to carry out the work which you have assigned to him effectively and efficiently. Grant him enduring faith humility and the strength of mind and body to continue to be a faithful and humble instrument in your hand for caring for the sick and suffering people of God and highlighting the true devotion to you, Our blessed Lord in the Holy Eucharist. We ask this through Christ Our Lord. Amen. V. St. John Mary Vianney, Patron of priests

R. Pray for him..

For the Superior General and her Councilors O Lord and Father of Our Saviour, we beg you to bless these your handmaids, Mother................and her councilors to whom you have entrusted this task of shepherding your children, the Sisters of Jesus Saviour. Enlighten them with your Holy Spirit, so that they may be able to govern and discern according to Your holy will. We ask this through Christ Our Lord.

Amen..

For Ourselves O Holy Spirit our Sanctifier, may I be immersed wholly and entirely in You. Be my Vivifier, Sanctifier and Unifier. Brighten my ways and give me the life and protection that Jesus the Saviour has by His death and resurrection gained for those who love Him. O Spirit Divine, may You never abandon me. Guide me strictly in the path of holiness so that I will be finally and firmly united to the Three Persons of the Blessed Trinity. O Blessed Virgin Mary, Spouse of the Holy Spirit and Mother of Jesus the Saviour, pray for me. V. Jesus the Saviour

R. Have mercy on us and grant us success.
