A BRIEF HISTORY OF THE SISTERS OF JESUS THE SAVIOUR

The Sisters of Jesus the Saviour is a young growing religious Congregation, which was founded by Rev. Fr. Prof. Emmanuel Matthew Paul Edeh C.S.Sp in the year 1985 in Port Harcourt Diocese.

The Sisters of Jesus the Saviour Congregation, otherwise known as, the Saviour Sisters (SJS) are based in Elele in the South-East of Nigeria. The Congregation was founded for a particular need and purpose in the life of the Church.

Our father founder always felt compassion for the sick and suffering people of God. He loves to stay with them to relieve their suffering, thus he scarcely had time for himself. These people, the sick, the mentally deranged, the less privileged and abandoned always gather around him to be helped. As they increased rapidly, he saw the need for helpers.



Thus he decided to found a Congregation of the Sisters to live and work in the midst of these sick and suffering people, the abjectly poor

and abandoned, so that the sisters could understand them, feel with them and manifest the charism of the founder in a profound manner. The sisters in humility try to portray the compassionate Jesus in their actions.

They profess the three evangelical counsels of poverty, chastity and obedience. The first intakes of this Congregation (26 in number) were screened and trained by the Sisters of Immaculate Heart of Mary Congregation (IHM),
with Sr. John Bechmans Ibekwe (IHM) as the first Novice Mistress assisted by four others. After the meticulous Postulancy period, followed by protracted and well organized Novitiate formation, the thirteen pioneer members of this young Congregation made their first religious profession on January 10th 1990. From then till now the Congregation has been having temporal and final professions yearly without any gap.

For Our Father Founder Under thy care O Lord, we entrust our father founder. Very Rev. Fr. Emmanuel M.P Edeh. Please Lord shower your unending blessing on Him, so that he will be able to carry out the work which you have assigned to him effectively and efficiently. Grant him enduring faith humility and the strength of mind and body to continue to be a faithful and humble instrument in your hand for caring for the sick and suffering people of God and highlighting the true devotion to you, Our blessed Lord in the Holy Eucharist. We ask this through Christ Our Lord. Amen. V. St. John Mary Vianney, Patron of priests

R. Pray for him..

For the Superior General and her Councilors O Lord and Father of Our Saviour, we beg you to bless these your handmaids, Mother................and her councilors to whom you have entrusted this task of shepherding your children, the Sisters of Jesus Saviour. Enlighten them with your Holy Spirit, so that they may be able to govern and discern according to Your holy will. We ask this through Christ Our Lord.

Amen..

For Ourselves O Holy Spirit our Sanctifier, may I be immersed wholly and entirely in You. Be my Vivifier, Sanctifier and Unifier. Brighten my ways and give me the life and protection that Jesus the Saviour has by His death and resurrection gained for those who love Him. O Spirit Divine, may You never abandon me. Guide me strictly in the path of holiness so that I will be finally and firmly united to the Three Persons of the Blessed Trinity. O Blessed Virgin Mary, Spouse of the Holy Spirit and Mother of Jesus the Saviour, pray for me. V. Jesus the Saviour

R. Have mercy on us and grant us success.
